The world advises, “Do it on your own.” Christ says, “I’ll do it for you.” The world suggests, “Advance by domination.” Christ says, “Advance by service.” The world chants, “Seize it now.” Christ says “Wait for the kingdom to come.” The world cries, “Vengeance!” Christ whispers, “Forgiveness!” The world says, “All roads lead to heaven.” Christ says, “I am the way, and the truth, and the life!” The world calls standard plays, resulting in brokenness and separation.
